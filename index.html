Kay Wasil

Kay is a multidisciplinary artist based in Brooklyn, NY, working in graphic design, physical computing, costume design, and performance. They are currently studying at NYU’s Interactive Telecommunications graduate program and hold a Bachelor of Fine Arts in Illustration and a minor in Art History from the Maryland Institute College of Art. They’ve shown work at Light Grey Art Lab and the Society of Illustrators, were a 2021 City Artist Corps grant recipient, and have appeared in events at venues such as The Brick, House of Yes, Club Cumming, Soho Playhouse, The Cell, Bizarre, and Chelsea Music Hall.

They can be reached at kay.wasil (at) nyu.edu
